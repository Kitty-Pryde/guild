Rift Walkers OOC
This is the OOC thread for the Rift Walkers (Book I)

The IC thread can be found here.

I'll approve characters here, here's the deal with the thread, though;

The worlds are falling apart, the very fabric of their existance being torn apart and portals joining them together, now, I'm allowing just about any race here, so, yeah, if you can get a race approved by me, then you're good to go. Now remember folks, you can by just about any race you want!

Now, the storyline will be something along the lines of us, either by accident or by choice of our own have taken a trip through these rifts, we'll all eventually meet, and go after the cause of the rifts, this, I have decided will be small crystals, about the size of a grown man's palm, they'll all be guarded by monsters, now, there's a twist to the crystals that will be revealed later on in the story.

These monsters might not necessarily be defeatable with common methods such as simple slaughter.

Now, here's your bio, everyone needs to fill this out for approval;

Name:
Age:
Race:
Race Description:
Gender:
Personality:
Appearance:
History (Not needed):
Equipment:
Magic (If applicable):
Original world:
Anything else:

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
It's called "Book I" to leave it open for a sequel, should public demand wish it to be so. (Or if I feel like it)
-------------------------------------------------
Here's mine;

Name: Drek'tar Glitterwing
Age: 24
Race: Vek'nari (Fairy)
Race Description: Tiny people who are adept at magic, they're physically frail, though they're your typical little winged mischief makers, they wear leaves, flowers and other plantlife as clothes, they're at one with nature, but despite that tend to be quite destructive with their magic, throwing fireballs and the like around.
Gender: Male
Personality: Withdrawn and determined, doesn't tend to talk much, though seems to be always caught up in deep thought.
Appearance: A tiny, golden-eyed male fairy dressed in a long-sleeved shirt created from an orange leaf, his pants are made from a dark green leaf. His wings are a deep, transparent red hue, almost blood-red, though, a little lighter in hue. His brown hair is short, reaching to the middle of his neck in messy locks. Stands at about 15 centimetres tall.
History (Not needed): Drek'tar is the only child of a long line of red-winged Vek'nari, his line is blessed with the destructive powers of fire, and weak healing powers, his family was never truly liked among the Vek'nari, but they didn't care, they could destroy the others should they choose to, but they are a line of peaceful fairies, not particularly caring for the matters of others, and barely ever going outside of their own business.
Equipment: N/A
Magic: Fire magic and weak healing magic, not so powerful, but powerful enough.
Original world: Sel'nar (The fairy world)
Anything else: N/A
