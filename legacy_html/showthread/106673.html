BACKGROUND
Spoiler

Tenshi Jigoku Academy was made for the purpose of educating the supernatural, the mythical, and the superhuman to blend in with the unknowing human world. However, even with the school's good intentions, chaos is abundant in these halls. It seemed to begin with the outbreak of a dark race called the Shadow Ghosts, who were controlled by thier Queen, the first headmaster, Alice. While posing as the Reaper King, her school was destroyed by an eccentric local diety, she was found out and once the school was rebuilt the throne was passed to a powerful but arrogant demon named Adian. Adian was part of a secret orginization called Wonderland, a small group of powerful demons and witches who's evil intent is unknown.

We last left the residents of TJA on thier field trip to Florida. Upon thier return, the members of TJA found thier school in shambles after being visited by two more forces of darkness; A malicious emporess from another demension and a mysterious legion of rougue supernaturals. Shortly after most of the wreckage and fatalities were picked up after, the headmaster and the entire orginization of Wonderland vanished, leaving the school in anarchy. For quite a while no one knew how to keep the academy going, to the point it was nearly shut down, until one man stepped up to claim the title of headmaster; Sin Sieben, a catboy who just happened to be god of the universe. He sent everyone off on thier merry way for a week, assuring all he would rebuild the school in 7 days.

Now in the month of June, the students enter the grounds. The original students return and the new come to enroll. But few know what's in store for them, for the academy is crawling with secret groups, sinister plots and hidden terrors.


SUMMARY
Spoiler

Tenshi Jigoku Academy welcomes all races, monster, yokai, human, you name it! Students live on the grounds, which is no inconvinence due to the school's massive and luxurious campus. Of course there are classes through the week, but they are not mandatory, nothing is really as long as the headmaster says so.

As a student you're free to wander around, make sexy time, and naturally since Sin is such a responsible headmaster, fighting is allowed. In fact it happens frequently. Basically students can enjoy lives with friends, lovers, mortal enemies, but one must always remember this is not your average institute. The humans enrolled here usually don't know about the supernatural word around them, and even the creatures have things to fear. Rumors spread of orginizations and evil hidden in plain sight; the shadow ghosts possibly returning, the rampid ballistic ghoul fueling people's fears, the demonic orginization looming on the sidelines, hell even people from other dimensions are targeting the academy!

Sign into our school and join the freakish and otherworldly as anything and everything can happen.
