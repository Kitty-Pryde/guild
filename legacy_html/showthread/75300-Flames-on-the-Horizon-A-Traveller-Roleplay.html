    Flames on the Horizon: A Traveller Roleplay.

    Interest Check ← Details Inside

    Keldari II, home of XX-Ball and the Red Scorpions Mercenaries, is the world you currently call home. Her Highport is the most advanced in the sector, headquartering the 2nd Imperial Fleet, alongside a dozen different corporations jockying for control of the surface. The Red Scorpions do these corporations' dirty work, with surgical strikes and other violent acts that constantly shift the power balance below. Your characters, ideally, are in the Yellow Zone, the area east-southeast of the orbital elevators, home of the Palace and Downport. Imperial troops guard the area, and Red Scorpions stay well-away from the zone. The Red Zone, encompassing the remainder of the city, is many different factories fought over daily between the megacorporations. Your story starts here.

    The Caliente Sol, a Far Trader with more history than your whorish aunt and most music groupies. She started out in service with the Red Scorpions, for Ortillery and aerial drops. She was taken from them by the Imperials when the 2nd was sent to restore order. She was pressed into scout service, helping locate Pirates and other threats across the local sector. After a few months in service, she took a direct hit to her engines, and was towed for scrapping. The governor of Keldari II ordered it repaired, and had it fitted as a personal luxury craft. Three years later, he retired it to his personal downport's hangars. It is, through our actions, that we acquire this ship.

    The Chateau D'Royeaux is a high-class luxury facility regularly visited by the Governor. It boasts XX-Ball in total zero-gravity environs, a firing range, and dozens of other amenities. On the lower levels, the Bloated Float is a regular low-class tavern. While ideally to make the nobility more in-touch, the real purpose is to get “fellows” for the Nobility to use agains the Megacorps and other organizations.

    ((In Traveller fashion, i'll let ya'll connect the dots. One of you can be a noble getting a team together, or perhaps the PCs steal the Sol from the Governor while working for him? I like giving choice to my players. Discuss here, por favor.))

    CS: ((In order of importance.))

    Name: Que es tu nombre, Hombre?

    Race: QUERY: What type of Meatbag are you?

    Age: For health reasons, we don't accept those over fifty five.

    Career(s): What've you done with every pitiful year of your life since schooling?

    Skills: Oh, you can do things? Like what?

    Biography: Tell me your story. I might just like it.

    Equipment: Adios Mios, what is that piece of junk?

    Appearance: My eyesight must be failing me, what do you look like?

    My personal Character

    Name: Jarrek Silverpelt

    Race: Vargr

    Age: 30

    Career(s): Scout (survey) 2 terms, Merchant (Free Trader) 1 term

    Skills: Pilot (spacecraft) 1, Zero-gravity 0, Vacc Suit 0, Broker 0, Engineer (J-drive) 0, Mechanic 1, Diplomat 0

    Biography: Jarrek grew up on an industrial asteroid in Imperial space, most of his pack were workers but he felt dissatisfied with that life so he joined the military as soon as possible. He spent seven years in the Scout corps surveying new planets and occasionally making First Contact, until his right arm was hacked off by a TL 2 primitive with a farming implement. Using his mustering out pay to buy a basic prosthetic he started working for a free trader crew as a pilot until he had saved enough credits and ship shares to try and make a down payment on his own ship.

    Equipment: Gauss pistol, neural comm (TL 13, computer has Intrusion 1 and Security 1), wafer jack, data wafers with Gun Combat (slug pistols) 1, Medic 1, Languages; HEV suit, 2500cr.

    Appearance: Appears to be a dark-furred anthropomorphic wolf with white highlights. Right arm is a basic prosthetic (no augmentation) covered with gleaming plastic. Access ports of his neural comm and wafer jack are barely visible on the left and right sides of the back of his head. Usually wears a black trenchcoat and gloves.
